Legends & Their Powers

Tank

The unbreakable frontline—wades into the fray, shrugging off blows and crushing foes in close combat.

The relentless storm—rains down rockets and homing fire, carving battlefields with precision.

The silent reaper—delivers piercing shots from afar, sets cunning traps, and calls in aerial support.

The life-binder—commands toxic clouds, bends enemies to your will, and raises the fallen as allies.
