📝 Changelog

```
Shoot’em All!: Backup Clones Ability v1.3.2 · Today

Added “Backup” ability: mirror‐clones roam randomly around the player, shooting nearby enemies with buckshot. Upgrades increase clone count and damage multiplier.

Shoot’em All!: Player Damage Fix v1.3.1 · Today

Fixed an issue where player took no damage from any source. Boss bullets and enemy collisions now correctly reduce health.

Shoot’em All!: “Insanity” ability + rainbow trail v1.3.0 · Today

New toggle skill that multiplies damage, doubles speed, cycles player colour and leaves a fading rainbow trail while draining 5 HP / s. Upgrades scale the multiplier from ×3 → ×11.

Shoot’em All!: Blade Orbit rework & skill-point system v1.2.9 · 2 days ago

Level-up now grants skill points and emerald arrows to upgrade abilities. Blade Orbit gains +1 blade and +35 DMG each rank (max 6 blades).
```
